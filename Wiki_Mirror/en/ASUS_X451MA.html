Specifications

  • CPU: Intel(R) Celeron(R) CPU N2815 @ 1.86GHz (dual core)
  • RAM: DDR3 1066 MHz 4096 MB
  • Screen: 14.0"
  • Hard Disk: Western Digital Blue WDC WD5000LPVX-80V0TT0 500GB SATA
  • Graphics: Intel Corporation Atom Processor Z36xxx/Z37xxx Series Graphics & Display
  • Wireless: Broadcom Inc. and subsidiaries BCM43142 802.11b/g/n (with Bluetooth)
  • Ethernet: Realtek Semiconductor Co., Ltd. RTL810xE PCI Express Fast Ethernet controller
  • DVD-RW

Drivers

Wireless

Brightness
