RiseupVPN is an easy, fast, and secure VPN service from riseup.net. RiseupVPN does not require a user account, keep logs, or track you in any way. The service is paid for entirely by donations from users like you.

Installation

Install riseup-vpnAUR or riseup-vpn-gitAUR from the AUR. There is also a snap package available.
