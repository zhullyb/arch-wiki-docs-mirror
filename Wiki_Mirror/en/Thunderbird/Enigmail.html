Enigmail is a Thunderbird extension that allows writing and receiving email signed and encrypted with the OpenPGP standard.

Enigmail functionality is integrated into Thunderbird since version 78.2.1, released on 2020-08-29.[1][2]

To migrate your keys and settings from Enigmail/GnuPG to Thunderbird, install the thunderbird-extension-enigmail package. If you did not use Enigmail in Thunderbird 68 or earlier, then this version of Enigmail will not do anything.
