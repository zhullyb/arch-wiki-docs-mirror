Summary

The 2nd generation of ASUS B9450 with 11th Gen Intel CPU with Intel Xe graphics.

Models are B9450CEA-* compared to B9450FA-* of the 1st generation.

BIOS

To boot from a USB stick, you may have to turn off Fast Boot and Secure Boot: How to boot the system from USB flash drive/CD-ROM.

Note: You don't have to turn off VMD in BIOS as long as you have vmd module enabled in your initram image [1].
Warning: Even though the model line is B9450CEA, in BIOS and on the official support page it's indicated as B9400CEA.

dmidecode will also display Product Name as ASUS EXPERTBOOK B9400CEA_B9450CEA and BIOS Version as, e.g., B9400CEA.301.

Hardware

I own both Core i7-1165G7 B9450CEA-XH75 (16G RAM/1TB SSD) and B9450CEA-XH77 (32G RAM/2TB SSD), so all hardware is the same apart from RAM/SSD size.

Audio

Recent versions of 2 packages are required for proper audio functioning: sof-firmware and alsa-ucm-conf.
