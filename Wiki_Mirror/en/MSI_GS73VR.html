Function keys

Only Fn+F4 (P1 key) and Fn+F5 (ECO key) needs to be remapped, other Fn keys work out of the box.

Fn+F7 (SHIFT key) and Fn+F10 (Airplane Mode) cannot be recognized. 

Fn keys Scancodes
0xef # P1 key
0xce # ECO key
