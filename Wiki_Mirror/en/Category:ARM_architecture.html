Note: For the repository of old packages formerly known as the Arch Rollback Machine, see Arch Linux Archive.

Arch Linux ARM is a port of Arch Linux to the ARM architecture. Pages in this category provide some additional tips and tricks for various ARM devices. They are not intended as a replacement for the official documentation, but were contributed before the Arch Linux ARM project restarted its own ALARM wiki. Further, note not all devices in this category are officially supported platforms of the ALARM project, which has to be selective with its resources.

For new articles, please consider first whether you can help the Arch Linux ARM project's wiki to a good start with your ARM specific contributions. Please also consider a duplication of topics between both wikis is counter-productive for all involved. If you create an article for a new topic on the ALARM wiki, you are welcome to create a short article redirecting the reader to it in this category.
