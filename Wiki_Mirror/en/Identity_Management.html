Introduction and Concepts

Identity managmenten (IDM), sometimes als identity and access management (IAM) deals with how users gain a digital identity, the roles, and sometimes the permission granted to this identity.

Software

There is a number of software which helps doing identity management. Amongst, in alphabetical order, tools and related:

  • Initiative for Open Authentication
