Железо

  • Процессор:
    • Microprocessor 1.60 GHz Intel Core processor i7-720QM
    • Microprocessor Cache 6 MB Level 2 cache
  • ОЗУ: 2 slots - ships with 2x2GB DDR3
  • Видео:
    • NVIDIA GeForce GT 230M, Up to 2815 MB (1 GB dedicated)
  • Дисплей: 17.3” Diagonal HD+ High-Definition HP LED BrightView Widescreen Display (1600 x 900)
  • Мультимедиа:
    • Звук:

Intel Corporation 5 Series/3400 Series Chipset High Definition Audio (Intel HDA)

nVidia Corporation High Definition Audio Controller (nVidia HDA)

    • Колонки: Altec Lansing with SRS Premium Sound and Sub-woofer (?)
    • Web-камера: integrated HP webcam
  • Сеть:
    • Wireless: Broadcom Corporation BCM4312 802.11b/g LP-PHY
    • Ethernet: Realtek Semiconductor Co., Ltd. RTL8111/8168B PCI Express Gigabit Ethernet controller
